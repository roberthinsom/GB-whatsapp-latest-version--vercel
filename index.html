2. Advanced Privacy Settings in GB WhatsApp

2.1 Hide Last Seen

One of the first privacy settings to consider is the ability to hide your "Last Seen" status. This feature allows you to go incognito and not reveal when you were last active.

  • How to Enable:
    • Open GB WhatsApp latest version and go to Settings.
    • Select Privacy Settings.
    • Toggle the Hide Last Seen option to On.

2.2 Disable Read Receipts

Read receipts can be a double-edged sword. While they inform senders when their messages are read, they can also lead to pressure and misunderstandings.

  • How to Disable:
    • In the Privacy Settings, find Disable Read Receipts.
    • Toggle this option to On.

2.3 Hide Online Status

If you want to maintain your privacy while still using the app, hiding your online status can be a game changer.

  • How to Hide:
    • Navigate to Privacy Settings.
    • Look for the Hide Online Status option and toggle it to On.

2.4 Control Who Can See Your Profile Photo

Profile photos are another aspect of your account that you may want to control. The GB WhatsApp latest version allows you to restrict who can see your profile picture.

  • How to Adjust:
    • Go to Privacy Settings.
    • Select Who Can See My Profile Photo and choose from options like Everyone, My Contacts, or Nobody.

2.5 Manage Status Viewers

Your status updates can also be kept private by managing who can view them.

  • How to Control Status Viewers:
    • In the Privacy Settings, find Who Can See My Status.
    • Select the desired audience for your status updates.
